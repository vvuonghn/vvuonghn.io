Vuong Ho is a master’s student in Computer Science at the University of Arkansas, specializing in Computer Vision and Graphics. His focus includes Generative Models, Diffusion Models, 3D Gaussian Splatting, and Large Language Models (LLMs). With three years of industry experience, he worked on perception systems for autonomous driving, contributing to 360-degree view reconstruction, camera calibration, 3D vision, and depth estimation.

Vuong’s research has been presented at MICCAI and ACCV, and he holds a patent with the World Intellectual Property Organization, showcasing his innovation in computer vision and autonomous systems.
